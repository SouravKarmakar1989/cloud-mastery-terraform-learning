Welcome to a new section of the course. In this section, we're going to focus on two of the important transformations in Spark. It's more likely in any data project, that we have more data than we need and we need to filter the data required for us. Spark gives you the Filter Transformation to achieve this. Similarly, the data received usually is spread across multiple tables of files. We would have to bring them together to make sense of the data and obtain business value. Spark provides a rich set of Join Transformation to achieve this. We're going to learn about these two transformations. Once we have learned about them, we'll put them into practice in our project. We'll bring together data from five different tables to create one table that could help us with analysis and reporting further down. By the end of this section, I hope you will gain a good understanding about Filter and Join Transformations and also the scenarios in which to use a specific type of Join Transformation. So let's get started.
