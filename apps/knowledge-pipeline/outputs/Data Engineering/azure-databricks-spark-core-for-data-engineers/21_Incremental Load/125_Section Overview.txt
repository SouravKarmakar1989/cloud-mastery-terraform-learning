Welcome to a new section of the course. So far, we have been building our project to take all of the data and overwrite the entire Data Lake on every execution. This is okay for the size of our data sets, but this won't be the case for most projects in the real world. We may have to use other design patterns. In this section, we'll discuss various Data Loading design patterns, such as full refresh, incremental load and hybrid approaches. We'll then discuss the approach we want to take for our project, which has been set up to use a hybrid scenario to provide maximum learning. Finally, we will apply these techniques to our project. By the end of this section, I hope you will have a good understanding about the various design patterns being used in the industry for loading data and also how to implement them in projects. Let's get started.
