Welcome to the section on Governance and Quality Assurance, where we shift from building generative AI systems to ensuring they are safe, reliable, well governed and thoroughly evaluated in real world deployments. Strong governance and continuous QA are just as important as model performance, and in this section you'll learn how to implement both using AWS native tools and best practices. We'll start with Bedrock Prompt Management, a centralized way to control version and audit the prompts your applications depend on. You'll also explore Bedrock agent tracing, which provides visibility into how agents reason, take actions, and interact with tools. Next, we'll dive into evaluation techniques for foundation models, including how to measure accuracy, relevance, consistency, and safety. You'll learn how to evaluate Llms using both manual and automated approaches, and how to apply bedrock model evaluations and other metrics to make data driven decisions. We'll also look at how to evaluate the quality of your Rag pipelines, ensuring your retrieval and augmentation steps deliver the right context at the right time. From there, we'll move into deployment validation systems and responsible AI. Covering how to test your AI systems for drift, hallucination risks and policy compliance before and after going live. The second half of this section focuses on observability. You'll learn how to use CloudWatch metrics, logs, and alarms to monitor model behavior, latency, token usage, and user interactions. We'll explore other AI monitoring tools, and you'll see how CloudTrail helps you track access patterns and maintain Auditability across your AI stack. We'll also cover AWS X-ray for tracing, Foundation model API calls, end to end and lake formation for governing access to the data that powers your models and vector stores. By the end of this section, you'll know how to implement comprehensive governance, monitoring, and QA strategies that keep your generative AI solutions trustworthy, compliant, and production ready. Let's get started.
