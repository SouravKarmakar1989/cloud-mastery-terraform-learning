# Protecting Your Data in Amazon S3

## Pointwise No-Loss Summary

1. Section shifts from access control to data-protection controls.
2. Protection focus addresses multiple failure/threat classes:
   - human mistakes,
   - malicious actions,
   - regional/geographical disruptions.

## Core Protection Themes Introduced

1. Encryption options for data confidentiality.
2. Versioning for accidental overwrite/delete resilience.
3. Replication for cross-location durability/availability goals.
4. MFA delete and object lock for stronger destructive-action controls.

## Why This Layer Matters

1. Access control alone is insufficient for complete data risk management.
2. Data-protection controls are needed for recovery, continuity, and compliance posture.

## Outcome

1. Learner receives roadmap of S3-native data-protection mechanisms covered in this module.
