# Cross-Region Replication

## Pointwise No-Loss Summary

1. Cross-Region Replication (CRR) copies objects between buckets in different AWS regions.
2. It supports geographic separation for risk, compliance, and performance goals.

## Why Use CRR (As Discussed)

1. Compliance/data-placement requirements needing distance or regional diversification.
2. Performance optimization by placing copies closer to distributed users/workloads.
3. Resiliency/business continuity through secondary regional copy.

## Operational Note

1. CRR is rule-driven replication between source and destination buckets in distinct regions.

## Outcome

1. Learner can map CRR to regulatory, latency, and disaster-resilience use cases.
