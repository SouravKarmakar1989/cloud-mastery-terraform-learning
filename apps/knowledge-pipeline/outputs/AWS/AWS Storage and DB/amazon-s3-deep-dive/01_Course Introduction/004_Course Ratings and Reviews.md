# Course Ratings and Reviews

## Pointwise No-Loss Summary

1. Instructor requests learners to leave course feedback.
2. Both positive and constructive reviews are welcomed.
3. Reviews are positioned as input for improving future course quality.
4. Reviews also help other students decide if course is a fit.
5. Suggested review content includes:
   - what was useful,
   - what was learned,
   - what could be improved.
6. Instructor thanks learners and encourages continued learning in future courses.
