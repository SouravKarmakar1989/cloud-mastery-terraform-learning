# Realizing Costs Benefits of Consolidated Billing

## Pointwise No-Loss Summary

1. Consolidated billing in AWS Organizations can improve effective pricing outcomes across multi-account estates.
2. Aggregated usage may unlock better tiered pricing versus isolated per-account billing views.

## Core Idea

1. Without consolidation, each account is priced on its own usage tier curve.
2. With consolidation, combined organizational usage is considered, potentially applying lower rates at higher aggregate tiers.

## Practical Benefit

1. Lower blended cost for high-volume S3 usage distributed across many accounts.
2. Centralized visibility and payment workflow through management account context.

## Outcome

1. Learner understands why multi-account organizations should leverage consolidated billing for S3 cost efficiency.
